# TOPIC 1: NUMBER SYSTEM

---

**Divisibility Rules**
- By 2 → last digit even
- By 3 → sum of digits divisible by 3
- By 4 → last 2 digits divisible by 4
- By 8 → last 3 digits divisible by 8
- By 9 → sum of digits divisible by 9
- By 11 → (odd position sum) − (even position sum) = 0 or multiple of 11
- By 6 → divisible by both 2 and 3
- By 12 → divisible by both 3 and 4

****LCM** & **HCF****
- **LCM** × **HCF** = Product of two numbers
- **HCF** of fractions = **HCF** of numerators / **LCM** of denominators
- **LCM** of fractions = **LCM** of numerators / **HCF** of denominators

**Sum Formulas**
- First n naturals = n(n+1)/2
- First n odd numbers = n²
- First n even numbers = n(n+1)
- Sum of squares = n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
- Sum of cubes = [n(n+1)/2]²

**Factors**
- If N = aᵖ × bᵍ × cʳ → Total factors = (p+1)(q+1)(r+1)
- Odd factors → ignore powers of 2, apply formula on rest
- Even factors = Total factors − Odd factors

**Remainders — Cyclicity**
- Powers of 2 mod 3: cycle = 2 (even power→1, odd→2)
- Powers of 7 mod 5: cycle = 4
- Last digit of 2ⁿ: cycle 4 → 2,4,8,6
- Last digit of 3ⁿ: cycle 4 → 3,9,7,1
- Last digit of 7ⁿ: cycle 4 → 7,9,3,1
- Last digit of 9ⁿ: cycle 2 → 9,1
- Last two digits of 7ⁿ: cycle 4 → 07,49,43,01

**Number of Digits**
- Digits in Nⁿ = ⌊n × log N⌋ + 1
- log 2 = 0.**3010**, log 3 = 0.**4771**, log 7 = 0.**8451**

**Key Identities**
- (a+b)² = a²+2ab+b²
- (a−b)² = a²−2ab+b²
- a²−b² = (a+b)(a−b)
- (a+b)³ = a³+3a²b+3ab²+b³
- a³+b³ = (a+b)(a²−ab+b²)
- a³−b³ = (a−b)(a²+ab+b²)
- If a+b+c=0 → a³+b³+c³ = 3abc

# TOPIC 5: PERCENTAGE

---

**Core**
- % = (Part/Whole) × **100**
- Increase: New = Original × (1+x/**100**)
- Decrease: New = Original × (1−x/**100**)

**Successive % Change**
- Net = a + b + ab/**100**
- Two equal changes ±x% → net = −x²/**100** (always loss)

**Reverse Percentage**
- Original = Final × **100**/(**100** ± change%)
- After 20% increase → Original = Final × **100**/**120**
- After 15% decrease → Original = Final × **100**/85

**Consumption & Expenditure**
- Price up x% → reduce consumption by x/(**100**+x) × **100**%
- Price down x% → increase consumption by x/(**100**−x) × **100**%

**Salary & Savings**
- Savings = Income − Expenditure
- If x% spent → (**100**−x)% saved
- Income = Savings × **100**/(**100**−x)
- % change in savings = (New savings − Old savings)/Old savings × **100**

**Election**
- Margin = (Winner% − Loser%) × Total votes / **100**
- Total = Margin / (difference in %)
- With invalid votes → find valid votes first, then scale up

**Population**
- After n years = P × (1+r/**100**)ⁿ
- Mixed rates → multiply each year's factor

**Marks & Exam**
- Pass marks = Max marks × Pass%/**100**
- If fails by x → scored = Pass marks − x
- Two students: equate pass marks, solve for Max

**Key Fraction-% Conversions**
- 1/6=16.67%, 1/7=14.28%, 1/8=12.5%, 1/9=11.11%, 1/11=9.09%, 1/12=8.33%

# TOPIC 6: PROFIT & LOSS

---

**Core**
- Profit = SP − CP, Profit% = Profit/CP × **100**
- Loss = CP − SP, Loss% = Loss/CP × **100**
- SP = CP × (**100**+P%)/**100**
- CP = SP × **100**/(**100**+P%)

**Markup & Discount**
- SP = MP × (1−d/**100**)
- MP = CP × (1+markup/**100**)
- Profit% when markup m%, discount d% = [(1+m/**100**)(1−d/**100**) − 1] × **100**

**Successive Discounts**
- Net discount = a+b − ab/**100**
- SP = MP × (1−d1/**100**) × (1−d2/**100**)

**Two Articles Same SP**
- Always loss = x²/**100** % (where x = common profit/loss %)

**Dishonest Dealer**
- Profit% = (True weight − False weight)/False weight × **100**
- With multiple effects (false weight + markup + discount) → calculate step by step

**CP of Multiple Articles**
- CP of m = SP of n → Profit% = (m−n)/n × **100**
- CP per unit = Total CP/m, SP per unit = Total SP/n

**Trader's Combined Profit**

- Sells fraction at x% profit, rest at y% loss
- Net% = fraction1 × x + fraction2 × (−y)

**SI = Principal**

**Core**

**Work & Wages**
- Wage ratio = work done ratio = (rate × time) for each

**Fraction of Work**
- Does x/y of work in d days → full work in d×y/x days

**Pipes & Cistern**

**Leak Problems**
- With leak rate = 1/fill − 1/leak

# TOPIC 27: AP & GP SERIES

**AP**

**GP**
- nth term = a×rⁿ⁻¹

**Series Type Detection**
- Constant difference → AP
- Constant ratio → GP
- Differences form AP → quadratic series
- Differences form GP → geometric-based series

- Cone: V=⅓πr²h, **CSA**=πrl, **TSA**=πr(r+l), l=√(r²+h²)
- Sphere: V=4/3πr³, SA=4πr²
- Hemisphere: V=2/3πr³, **CSA**=2πr², **TSA**=3πr²

# TRAP CHECKLIST (Common Mistakes)

---

- *A more than B by x%* → B less than A by x/(**100**+x)% **NOT** x%
- Half distance average speed → harmonic mean **NOT** arithmetic
- CI half-yearly → halve rate, double time
- BD always > TD for same bill
- Two articles same SP profit/loss → always loss formula
- Circular arrangement → (n−1)! not n!
- With replacement → same denominator, without → reduce by 1
- Remainder of even power of 2 mod 3 → always 1
- Leap year: **100**,**200**,**300** **NOT** leap. **400** IS leap
- Clock right angle → 22 times not 24 in 12 hours
- Partnership different times → Capital×Time, not just Capital
